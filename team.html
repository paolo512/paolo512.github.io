Paul Pillitteri – Lead, Design & Fabrication

Seasoned entrepreneur with an extensive background in hard and soft goods product creation, technology, art and music. Overall vision for the concept. Direction for all aspects of the planning, software, music, design and UI/UX. CNC programming and fabrication for finished structure. Hand picked team members, aligned the common vision and managed development, timelines and budgets for development. Managed relationship with San Francisco City Planning Department and Festival staff. LinkedIn Profile

Seth Neiman – Advisor & Technology

Entrepreneur, technologist, investor and professional sportscar driver with a passion for music and incubating companies with world-changing business models. Provided advice and design input during all stages. Wrote software to take input from the iPad cameras, interface with the expression detection engine from Emotient and emulate controllers to drive Ableton Live.

Josh Coleman – Community & Composer

Josh Coleman was admitted to study composition at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music under Pulitzer Prize winning composer John Adams. His music has been used on NBC Chicago Fire, the Discovery Channel, CW, and Bravo. He has also composed music for local plays and the documentary Center of the Storm. He created original compositions for the installation. Learn more here.

Coleman is also an internationally recognized psychologist and author who appears frequently on The Today Show and NPR and has appeared on Sesame Street, 20/20, Good Morning America and many other media outlets. He is Co-Chair of the Council on Contemporary Families. Learn more here.
